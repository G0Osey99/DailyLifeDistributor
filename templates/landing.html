Workflow

Pick folders, match dates, run. The rest is automatic.

Your media stays on your machine. The browser streams just the dates being worked on, in chunks; the VPS never holds more than a few days' files at once.

/ 01
Pick folders & sheet
Horizontal, vertical, podcast, thumbnails, and your planning .xlsx — chosen with the browser folder picker.
/ 02
Match dates
The server parses filenames in YYMMDD / DDMMYY / MMDD and aligns them against rows in your sheet.
/ 03
Choose dates & toggles
Tick the dates and platforms for this run. Per-element defaults come from your config.
/ 04
Run & watch
Per-row progress streams over SSE. Email rows wait for their YouTube watch URL automatically.
Why teams use it

Built for the operator who manages a daily devotional and never wants to think about uploads.

Excel is the source of truth
Map columns once. Titles, descriptions, tags, scripture, prayer and topic all come from your planning sheet — no copy/paste, no drift.
Resumable runs
Every attempt lands in SQLite. If a batch crashes, re-run the rest — succeeded dates are skipped automatically.
Live progress over SSE
Bytes uploaded, processing phase, success / error — per row, per platform, per date. No spinners that just spin.
Self-hosted & encrypted
Run locally with python app.py, or behind your VPS. All platform secrets live in a Fernet-encrypted store on disk.
Local LLM title suggestions
Feeds your transcript column into a locally-running model (llamafile or Ollama). No audio transcription, no extra upload.
Calendar & history view
See what's scheduled and what's published across all destinations on one calendar, with the full per-platform outcome trail in History.
